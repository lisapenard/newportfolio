A little about me...

I am a french UX/UI designer based in Paris. I have been studying webdesign for three years in the french greatest school of internet, HETIC and I will graduate in two years.
I have the chance to be multidisciplinary since I study equally web development, design, digital marketing, web analytics, copywriting and many other domains.


"Look at usual things with unusal eyes". I like this quote because I think it defines pretty well who I want to be and my objectives when it comes to my professional life.
I want to work with people that inspires me and learn as much as can from them. I believe that I can be a great asset to a team : I speak my mind and I take everyone's opinion into perspective in order to have the best result possible.
